About Blood Doners

One of the most necessary element of human body is blood. In our country, it is really complicated to find a donor at the time of need. We are trying to find a easy way of blood donation. People tries multiple ways to find a blood donor like they post on social media, contact with blood donation group, relatives and friends etc. But in various times they are unable to manage blood. This project is based on blood donation in order to find blood donor easily in proper time at proper place. As a result the sufferings of people will be reduce. This website will hold various kinds of facilities like it display the details of blood donor, quantity of blood, the details and location of that person who requires the blood.
